---

## Who We Are
The Innovative Business Incubator Department of AZUAC is a university-based incubator that helps grow early-stage ideas through practical support, startup programs, mentoring, and access to innovation resources.

## Our Mission
To build a strong university innovation culture by helping students and academic staff develop entrepreneurial skills, create sustainable ventures, and connect with the wider startup ecosystem.

## What We Offer
- Startup incubation and pre-incubation programs
- Bootcamps and entrepreneurship training
- Mentoring and coaching
- Business model and business planning support
- Branding and market development support
- Access to grants and funding opportunities
- International networking opportunities
- Innovation and entrepreneurship ecosystem connections
- Support for social impact, green, and SDG-oriented initiatives

## Resources
Students and young entrepreneurs can benefit from:
- Access to university labs and research facilities
- Access to innovation labs and prototyping spaces
- Access to maker spaces, technical equipment, and design tools
- Technology transfer support and expert consultation
- Individual mentoring from academic staff and industry experts

## Programs
Our main activities include:
- Incubation programs
- Pre-incubation programs
- Acceleration support
- Startup bootcamps
- Business skills and financial literacy training
- Social innovation and sustainable business development activities

## How Students Can Join
Students can join by:
1. Following our open calls and announcements
2. Applying to our incubation and bootcamp programs
3. Submitting a startup idea, innovation concept, or early-stage project
4. Participating in training, mentoring, and ecosystem events
5. Developing their project with expert support

## Partner Opportunities
